Sports Analysis Project

Explore the T20 Cricket World Cup 2022 with 'T20 World Cup Insights,' a data-driven journey into every match, player, and team. The GitHub repository holds valuable data, cleaned and transformed, along with Power BI dashboards for easy insights. Discover winning strategies, standout player performances, and the stories that defined the tournament through detailed analysis and simple visualizations. Use reproducible workflows and well-documented code in the repository to explore and build upon our analysis, whether you're a cricket fan or a data enthusiast. Join us in unlocking cricket's stories hidden within the numbers, transforming the way we see and understand the game. 'T20 World Cup Insights' is your guide to cricket's finest moments using tools and technologies like GitHub, Power BI, and reproducible workflows.
